About Us

Sweet Heaven was born out of our collective love for sharing wonderful cakes and pastries with friends and family. Today the Sweet Heaven community is still committed to using the finest ingredients from all over the world ensuring that quality remains the cornerstone of the Sweet Heaven tradition. Founded in 1946, we have ensured that our recipes and high standards remain, including our motto "quality is the number one ingredient in everything we do." Come visit our bakery/café and you will experience home baking at its best.
